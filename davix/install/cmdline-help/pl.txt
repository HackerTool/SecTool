DAVIX Quick Start Help
----------------------


Example:        pl example.ploticus -o example.png


example.ploticus:
                #proc getdata
                file: data.ploticus
                fieldnames: time amount
                #proc areadef
                   xscaletype: time hh:mm:ss
                   xrange: 13:39:14 13:39:30
                   yrange: 0 30
                #proc xaxis
                   stubs: inc 2 seconds
                   minorticinc: 1 second
                   stubformat: ss
                #proc yaxis
                  stubs: inc 10
                  grid: color=orange
                #proc scatterplot
                xfield: time
                yfield: amount
